Our Story

Our founder Henry Nene was seen dancing when he used to work at a restaurant and the clients approached him asked him to teach their kids and that's how HHMZ was born

We have competed in Euro and America, and our Soloist in America came 1st and in 2023 we took 3 groups to Croatia and they all made top 10. We have won a lot competitions locally and done a few music videos and work with Co-operate events.

Break Dance

Unleash your inner B-boy or B-girl with our Break Dance classes! Break Dance, or B-boying/B-girling, is an explosive style that combines strength, agility, and creativity.



Our sessions are high-energy and packed with challenging moves like spins, freezes, and power moves.

Whether you're new to Break Dance or looking to refine your skills, our instructors are here to guide you every step of the way. Build your confidence, get fit, and become part of a vibrant community that celebrates individuality and skill.

Fun Fact: Break Dance will be featured as an official sport in the 2024 Paris Olympics!

Urban Dance

Experience the cutting-edge of dance with our Urban Dance classes! This style is a fusion of various street dance forms, bringing a raw, edgy, and contemporary feel to your movements.



Our classes focus on musicality, precision, and storytelling, allowing you to connect deeply with the music and your audience.

Urban Dance is perfect for those who love to experiment and push their boundaries. Our talented instructors will help you develop your unique style and bring out your inner performer.

Amapiano

Discover the smooth and soulful vibes of Amapiano with our dedicated dance classes! Originating from South Africa, Amapiano is characterized by its mellow beats and catchy rhythms.


Our classes offer a unique blend of traditional and modern dance elements, making it a fun and engaging style to learn.

Amapiano dance is perfect for anyone looking to explore new rhythms and movements. Our instructors will help you capture the essence of this beautiful dance style and make every class a memorable experience.

Fun Fact: Amapiano means "the pianos" in Zulu, highlighting the genre's piano-led sound!!

HipHop

Dive into the vibrant world of Hip-Hop, where every beat invites you to express your individuality! Our Hip-Hop classes are all about energy, creativity, and fun.

Frequently
Asked
Questions

How do i get started?

Getting started is easy! Email us at hmzdancesa@gmail.com , call the number on our Contact page, or fill out the contact form. We'll respond quickly to confirm your registration. Once everything is set, come in for your first class. We can’t wait to meet you!

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes, we offer trial classes! You can join a class to experience our teaching style and atmosphere before committing to a full session. Contact us to schedule your trial class today!

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. Athletic wear or dance attire is ideal. Don’t forget to bring appropriate dance shoes or sneakers, depending on the class style.

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer classes for everyone 4 years and older, from children to adults. Whether you're a young dancer starting out or an adult looking to learn something new, we have a class for you.
