Break Dance

Unleash your inner B-boy or B-girl with our Break Dance classes! Break Dance, or B-boying/B-girling, is an explosive style that combines strength, agility, and creativity.



Our sessions are high-energy and packed with challenging moves like spins, freezes, and power moves.

Whether you're new to Break Dance or looking to refine your skills, our instructors are here to guide you every step of the way. Build your confidence, get fit, and become part of a vibrant community that celebrates individuality and skill.

Amapiano

Discover the smooth and soulful vibes of Amapiano with our dedicated dance classes! Originating from South Africa, Amapiano is characterized by its mellow beats and catchy rhythms.


Our classes offer a unique blend of traditional and modern dance elements, making it a fun and engaging style to learn.

Amapiano dance is perfect for anyone looking to explore new rhythms and movements. Our instructors will help you capture the essence of this beautiful dance style and make every class a memorable experience.

Fun Fact: Amapiano means "the pianos" in Zulu, highlighting the genre's piano-led sound!!

Frequently
Asked
Questions

How do i get started?

Getting started is easy! Email us at hmzdanceschool@gmail.com , call the number on our Contact page, or fill out the contact form. We'll respond quickly to confirm your registration. Once everything is set, come in for your first class. We can’t wait to meet you!

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes, we offer trial classes! You can join a class to experience our teaching style and atmosphere before committing to a full session. Contact us to schedule your trial class today!

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. Athletic wear or dance attire is ideal. Don’t forget to bring appropriate dance shoes or sneakers, depending on the class style.

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer classes for all age groups, from children to adults. Whether you're a young dancer starting out or an adult looking to learn something new, we have a class for you.
